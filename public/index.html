Share a ride on your route only and earn easy bucks

Going to and fro from your office can now bring you money

If you have a car of your own and wish to offer rides to fellow passengers along your route then Rideo can help you earn money too in that process. Download our mobile app and you can choose when you wish to share your ride. Simply let us know when you are planning to start your trip and your source & destination.

We will match other riders who are looking for a ride along your route only and instruct them to stand at certain stop-over points along your path so that you don't get late. You get to select how many seats you wish to share and have rights to reject or end a trip if you are uncomfortable with any co-passenger. Share.Earn.Repeat
